# Privacy Policy – Scripture Verse Popup Preview ESV This extension does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally-identifiable information. - It runs entirely in your browser. - It reads only the text you actively select on the page. - It sends a Bible reference (e.g., “John 3:16”) to **bible-api.com** to retrieve the corresponding verse text. - No analytics, tracking, or advertising code is included. - No data are sold or shared with third parties.
